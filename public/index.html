Now and Then - The Beatles in 2023

Wow. Kind of an emotionally charged listen. As a long-time fan of the Beatles, I think the story behind this song is really touching, and to think that… A) I’m able to say that I was alive when the Beatles released “new music” (something that is causing some cognitive dissonance), and… B) that this is being called their “final song,” just makes me feel a bit overwhelmed. Truth be told, as a song, I would mostly say it was fine. I would probably consider it a less standout track for the Beatles had it been included on any of their previous albums.
